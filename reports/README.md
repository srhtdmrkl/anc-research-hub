# ANC Analytical Research Hub

This directory contains the comprehensive data science suite analyzing the Romanian National Citizenship Authority (ANC). The research is organized into three analytical layers: **Baseline Market Analysis**, **Operational Intelligence**, and **Predictive Future-State Modeling**.

---

## 1. Baseline Market & Demographic Analysis
*Establishing the "Ground Truth" of applicant volume and untapped global potential.*

- **[Article 10 Analysis](article_10_analysis_report.md)**: Deep dive into the restoration track for the global diaspora (Turk-Tatar, Jewish, German groups).
- **[Article 11 Analysis](article_11_analysis_report.md)**: Structural analysis of the high-volume re-acquisition track for territorial residents (Moldova/Ukraine).
- **[Cross-Article Analysis](cross_article_analysis_report.md)**: A comparative study of institutional throughput and systemic bottlenecks.
- **[Romanian Citizenship Saturation Analysis](saturation_report.md)**: Estimation of the "Global Addressable Market" (6M - 8M potential descendants) vs. current intake.

## 2. Operational Intelligence & Institutional Rhythms
*Mapping the internal gears, signature patterns, and resource trade-offs.*

- **[Institutional Rhythms](institutional_pulse_analysis.md)**: Identification of quarterly signature "flushes," mid-week peaks, and the December operational blackout.
- **[Resource Competition Analysis](resource_competition_analysis.md)**: A study of the correlation between Article 10 registrations and Article 11 throughput.
- **[Leadership Era Stress Test](leadership_stress_test.md)**: Ranking ANC administrations by their consistency and resilience to demand shocks (2012–Present).

## 3. Predictive Modeling & Outlier Studies
*Using ML and simulations to predict resolution speed and future structural shifts.*

- **[Fast-Track Predictors](fast_track_analysis.md)**: A Random Forest model identifying the institutional state variables that drive "one-year" resolutions.
- **[Orphaned Case Analysis](orphaned_cases_analysis.md)**: Characterizing the ~19,700 dossiers pending for >6 years.
- **[Law 14 Workload Modeling](law_14_realization_modeling.md)**: Projections on backlog composition following the B1 language requirement.

---
*All findings are based on a dataset of **>770,000 dossiers** spanning from 2012 to early 2026.*
